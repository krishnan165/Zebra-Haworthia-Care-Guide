Zebra Haworthia Care Guide

Soil for Zebra Haworthia

Well-draining succulent mix.

Zebra Haworthias thrive in well-draining soil designed for succulents, providing the necessary aeration and drainage.

Watering

Infrequent, deep watering.

Water Zebra Haworthias sparingly but deeply, allowing the soil to dry out between watering sessions to prevent root rot.

Lighting

Indirect sunlight or partial shade.

Place Zebra Haworthias in a location with indirect sunlight or partial shade to prevent sunburn on their unique striped leaves.

Benefits

Distinctive zebra-like patterns.

Zebra Haworthias are known for their striking, zebra-like patterns on their leaves, making them visually appealing.

Suitable for indoor environments.

These succulents adapt well to indoor conditions, adding a touch of nature to your home or office space.

Non-Benefits

Avoid overwatering; excessive moisture can lead to root problems and plant decline.
